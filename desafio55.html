Desafio 5/5

You and your friends will challenge yourselves: for 5 weeks, you'll each exercise 5 times a week. Anyone who doesn't has to pay up (and you can spend the money on the group after the 5 weeks are over).

On the app, you form the group, define your goals, post images of your workouts as proof, and keep track of everyone's scores.

Does it work?

I know it does. How? It worked for my friends and me. Now, it can work for you too.

  • Social incentives to actually get fit.
  • Fun with your friends and a reason to chat every day.
  • Actual accountability.
  • A fun weekend together spending the payouts.
