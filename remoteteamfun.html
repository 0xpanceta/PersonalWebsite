Remote Team Fun

It's easy to disengage from your work when you're fully remote. Especially if part of the team is remote, and part isn't.

Now, I won't solve your problem 100%, but I can definitely draw your team closer, with a touch of humanity. Here's wat Remote Team Fun does.

Whenever the birthday of a team member is coming up, we'll send everyone else an email asking for a few happy birthday words. We'll add in a few suggestions and prompts, which will make the message be even more touching.

When the actual date comes, we'll agther them up in a stylized birthday card and send them, along with the caring words of their friends.

The best part? As this is all in Beta stage, it's 100% free. Sign your team up today.

Could you do it by yourself?

Definitely. There are three problems with that approach, though:

  • It would take man power, which is definitely more expensive than th price I'd charge you (if/when I do).
  • Inconsistence: it'd be a bad look if someone was forgotten. The machine never forgets though.
  • Would you even do it? Let's face it, this might fall into the end of tasklist after tasklist and never get done.
