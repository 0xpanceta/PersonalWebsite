Influencer Success

An app that allows you to track and quantify the performance of every post/reel/story on instagram, so you can back up your strategy with numbers.

You'll avoid...

  • Having to randomly guess what's better and what's worse
  • Spending hours scrolling to understand what performs better
  • Wasting hours on content that used to work but is no longer engaging
  • Being the last one to find out that a line of content is outdated

Priority Level: Low

Ease to make: 2/5?

Meta's APIs are hard to get. Scraping I'm not so sure, but I don't know how it's done yet.

Ease to grow: 2/5?

Probably via instagram and TikTok. Would be fun to learn TikTok for this. Use Level's strategy of pre-launching on TikTok to see demand.

Ease to monetize: 4/5

Charge only from those who outsorce.
