Wedding Shepherd

You join and register your guests, straight from your phone contact list. They'll get a message saying they've been added to your wedding party.

You register all of your events, the information needed (e.g. specific times, addresses, dress code, and whatever else you need). Your guests will get a message early in the morning warning them about the day's events, so they'll be there in time, dressed appropriately.

You'll avoid...

  • tons of messages on your busiest day of the decade asking where the reception is (even after telling the guests 1000 times...)
  • or your friend's clueless new boyfriend showing up in red to an all-blue party and ruining the pictures
  • your sister-in-law, who's a bridesmaid, getting there two hours after the photo shooting started
  • grandma missing the most amazing toast because she "thought it was as 3pm"
